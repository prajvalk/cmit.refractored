Epiphany — The CMIT Mathematics Tournament.

Epiphany, the CMIT Mathematics Tournament is an annual pre-PhD level mathematics competition organised by the Club of Mathematics, IISER Thiruvananthapuram, in association with Anvesha.

This is a student-run contest aimed at fostering interest in mathematics and providing an exciting platform to students to assess their skills. Epiphany is conducted as a part of $\pi$-Day celebrations each year.

Epiphany 2.0: The Treasure Hunt

As part of our $\pi$ week celebrations, CMIT in collaboration with Science and Technology Council, IISER Thiruvananthapuram, is organising 'Epiphany' after a long hiatus. This time, Epiphany has revamped itself into a math-themed treasure hunt on Discord! You may participate in teams with a maximum of three members. The 1st place winners will be awarded a prize money of ₹3,141, and the 2nd place winners, ₹1,571. Further details of the hunt will be shared on Discord.

Epiphany 1.0: The Math Tournament

The first edition of Epiphany was conducted as an intra-IISER Mathematics Contest, open only to the students of IISER Thiruvananthapuram. The competition was be a three-hour long individual written exam based on undergraduate and pre-PhD level mathematics with two separate categories for: (I) Mathematics majors and (II) Foundation course students & other majors. Winners from each category won exciting cash prizes, sponsored by Anvesha, the institute science club.
